About me

Curious by nature and passionate about cross-cultural exploration, I’m Jasmine Tsui—a content creator and observer navigating life between Hong Kong and Switzerland. My story blends my Cantonese heritage, international experiences, and a drive to spark insightful conversations through podcasting and creative projects.

I’m constantly seeking fresh perspectives and learning opportunities, whether it’s experimenting with AI automation tools, sharing stories about societal differences, or building connections that transcend geographical boundaries. Beyond professional titles, I believe in continuous growth and self-driven innovation—bringing together storytelling, technology, and empathy in everything I do.

This website is where I share my journey, document my discoveries, and connect with others who value open-mindedness, creativity, and lifelong learning.
